What is 1 Millions community ?

1 Millions is erc20 that was built for the Blockchain community, has been supported by popular wallets on your computer, your smartphone, or websites like Mew, Metamask, Trustwallet, Imtoken and other erc20 wallets. One of the most popular uses for Giving Token is "tipping" fellow internet-goers who create or share great content. Think of it as a more meaningful "like" or upvote, with real value which can be used by the community.
